Well Construction · Liner Hangers

Set your liner flawlessly. No errors, no rework, pure magic.

One of the widest liner-hanger ranges from a single source — hydraulic-set, mechanical-set and specialized intelligent-setting designs, from offshore HP/HT wells to simple shallow wells.

Request the datasheet

The range caters to the widest range of conditions — from offshore HT/HP wells to simple shallow wells — with flawless ease. The patent-filed Equilibrium hydraulic hangers use pressure-balancers to separate the slip: the hanger sets only on a deliberate trigger, giving full confidence while you circulate and ream in HP/HT, high-consequence and extended-reach floating-rig wells.

Closed "wash-to-bottom" slips stay shrouded until set so you can ream past tight spots; a slim-OD low-clearance body handles restricted liner-to-casing annulus; and hydraulic setting suits running a new liner through an existing one or working from a floating rig. Backed by deep field experience delivering large downhole projects across Asia-Pacific, the Middle East and North Africa.
