Products & Services · Well Completion

A completion is where a well starts earning.

Eleven packer families from a simple mechanical set to intelligent, HP/HT and thermal designs, seventeen tubular accessories, and a full flow-control line.

The headline items show the ambition of an India-first startup — the MATI intelligent packer carries up to eight control-line feed-throughs for real-time monitoring and remote flow control; PARAM holds 15,000 psi to 450 °F; TAAP runs to 650 °F for steam and geothermal. Built to API / ISO 14310 targets and manufactured in-house, the range lets an operator configure a complete, monitored, high-integrity completion from a single domestic source.
