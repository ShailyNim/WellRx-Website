Well Construction · Casing & Cementing Accessories

Building well integrity you can drill through forever.

Every well's integrity is set at the cementing stage — get that job right and everything above it is sound. WellRx builds the full casing-and-cementing package in-house, tailored to the well's own design.

The operator sources one complete primary-cementing package from one Indian manufacturer, instead of matching mismatched imports joint by joint. Float equipment never lets the rig bear its full weight and holds off flowback once pumping stops; reamer shoes provide uniform cement coverage; baskets shield weak and lost-circulation zones; and stage tools place cement in two stages where one won't reach. PDC-drillable plugs and the floats drill out fast and clean, so nothing slows the well down.

Float Collars

PLAZR — Float Collars

Full range of check-valve types — flapper, spring-loaded ball and more — for dependable flowback prevention. Custom designs on request.

Float Shoes

PLAPAD — Float Shoes

Floats the casing into position so the rig needn't carry full string weight; guides to hole centre past ledges and washouts.

Centralizers

KENDR — Centralizers

Engineered for strength and standoff performance across every common casing/hole combination. Custom designs on request.

Reamer Shoes

AST — Reamer Shoes

Blade-and-diamond cutting faces get casing and liners safely to bottom while rotating and reciprocating past ledges, shale and sand bridges.

Stop Collars

VIRZ — Stop Collars

Matched to WellRx centralizers to lock in the standoff you want — set-screw, slip, nail or adhesive fastening for any application.

Cement Plugs

VAJR — Cement Plugs

Aluminium-insert-and-nitrile design lets the bit fracture rather than tear the element — fully PDC-drillable, no metal parts.

Cement Head

VAKA — Cement Head

Reliable sequenced launch of bottom and top plugs, with a built-in manifold for connecting the circulation line during the job.

Cement Basket

VAPE — Cement Basket

Shields weak formations and lost-circulation zones from cement-column pressure, and carries the column's weight once cementing stops.

Stage Tool

VEVAJ — Stage Cementing Tool

Proven, low-risk two-stage cementing — compact design with minimal moving parts, reliable and easy to handle.

Cement Retainer

VABHA — Cement Retainer

Dependable isolation for squeeze and remedial jobs — stinger-engaged, sealing off the wellbore below the moment the stinger is pulled.
