Project Tarajan — Field Development & Production Enhancement, Assam

WellRx's first turnkey exploration and production project, taken up in 2023 under the Government of India's Production Enhancement Contract (PEC) framework.

The mandate covered the full scope of a field revival: site development, well intervention, and the restoration of production from a group of wells that had been idle for over four decades.

Working with its own downhole intervention technology, WellRx re-entered and reinstated these legacy wells rather than drilling new ones — a lower-cost, lower-footprint route to renewed output. The intervention programme confirmed substantial untapped gas reserves within the field, establishing Tarajan as a valued asset and validating the case for extending the same approach to other mature and suspended wells across the region.
