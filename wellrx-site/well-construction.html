Products & Services · Well Construction

Well integrity you build once and produce through forever.

WellRx designs are inspired by decades of observing what goes wrong downhole in fields — where liners get stuck, where slips set early, where a hanger won't pass a tight annulus. We engineered for those exact moments.

Our patent-filed Equilibrium liner hangers are pressure-balanced to set only on a deliberate command. Closed "wash-to-bottom" slips stay shrouded until set; a slim-OD low-clearance body handles restricted liner-to-casing annulus.

WellRx builds the complete well-construction range in-house — hangers, liner-top packers, tie-back equipment, floats, landing collars, running tools, plus the full casing-and-cementing package — all designed and manufactured together. The operator sources one integrated system from one Indian manufacturer instead of stitching together mismatched imports joint by joint. One string, one source, no surprises.
